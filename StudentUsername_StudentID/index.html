Our Story

Here at care packages we aspire to help those in need by providing specially designed packs to help you with your needs whether you are in self isolation or just don't have the time to get up and get the essentials we have a package specially designed for you.

Our founders Lina Zhang, Paora Kairua and John Brown created Care Packages with the intention to provide non-perishable goods that will delight, entertain and provide delight in every Care Package sent out.

Our goal with each package is to ensure that it arrives on time to the designated giftee, we aim at providing sustainable and affordable packages that can cater to everyone's needs weather you are a teen living in hall of residence or a adult living in an apartment complex.
